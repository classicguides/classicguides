Addons

Here you'll find a list of useful & essential addons for Classic WoW (1.12).

Click the addon name to download

  • Questie - A standalone Classic QuestHelper.
  • Cartographer - Addon to manipulate in-game map & give you coordinates.
  • pfUI - Full UI replacement in one addon. The design is highly inspired by TukUI and ElvUI.
  • AtlasLoot Enhanced - Shows possible loot from dungeons, raids and much more.
  • Enemy Cast Bar - Shows a replication of your targets cast bar.
  • DPSMate - Damage Meter.
  • EquipCompare - Compare items easily with ones you have equipped.
  • CooldownCount - Allows you to see time until actions have cooled down in the buttons.
  • KLHThreatMeter - Threat meter.
  • Luna Unitframes - Unit Frame replacement, similar to ShadowedUnitFrames.
  • BigWigs - Boss Mods with timer bars for raiding.
  • CC Watch - Timer bars for CC & other abilities.
  • EzDismount - Automatically dismounts you when performing certain actions.
  • Bartender 2 - ActionBar AddOn which modifies the layout using buttons provided by Blizzard

How To Install:

  1. Download the addon by clicking the links above, then clicking the download button in the top-right corner.
  2. Extract the downloaded Zip folder. (ex: Questie.zip)
  3. Copy the new extracted folder (Questie) and paste it into World of Warcraft/Interface/Addons
  4. Restart WoW

All addons are downloaded directly from Google Drive.
